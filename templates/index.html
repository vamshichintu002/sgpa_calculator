SGPA Calculator

A lot of students studying in JNTUH Btech program may face difficulty in calculating their SGPA
(Semester Grade Point Average) based on their exam results.
To solve this problem, I have designed an SGPA calculator that simplifies the process of calculating your SGPA.
This calculator takes your credits and grades for each subject as input and then calculates your SGPA percentage, which can be extremely helpful for students who are trying to understand their academic performance.
